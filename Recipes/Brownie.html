Brownie Recipe

Description

A chocolate brownie, or simply a brownie, is a chocolate baked dessert bar. Brownies come in a variety of forms and may be either fudgy or cakey. the recipe we have today is for a cakey brownie.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Cocoa powder
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Vanilla extract
  • Baking powder
  • Plain flour

Now that your Ingredients are ready Let's Start Baking

Steps

  1. Prepare the oven and pan: Preheat the oven to 350Â°F and grease and flour an 8-inch square pan.
  2. Melt butter: Melt butter in a saucepan and remove from heat.
  3. Mix wet ingredients: Stir in sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
  4. Mix dry ingredients: Beat in cocoa, flour, salt, and baking powder.
  5. Combine wet and dry ingredients: Combine the wet and dry ingredients.
  6. Pour batter into pan: Spread the batter into the prepared pan.
  7. Bake: Bake until the top is dry and the edges pull away from the pan, which should take about 25 to 30 minutes.
  8. Cool: Let the brownies cool briefly before frosting
